About me

I'm now a member of Yanqi Lake Beijing Institute of Mathematical Sciences and Applications (BIMSA) as an assistant research fellow. Previously I was a postdoc in Max Planck Institute for Mathematics. I graduated from Tsinghua University in July 2019, under supervision of Eduard Looijenga .

I am interested in K3 surfaces, cubic fourfolds, hyper-Kahler manifolds, Calabi-Yau manifolds. Recently I worked on moduli and automorphisms of those objects.
