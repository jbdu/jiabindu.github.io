About me

I am currently a fourth year PhD candidate in School of Mathematical Sciences, Xiamen University, China. My PhD advisor is Prof. Wenfei Liu.

Now I am investigating the action of symplectic automorphisms of algebraic surfaces on the 0-th Chow group.

Publications & Preprints
